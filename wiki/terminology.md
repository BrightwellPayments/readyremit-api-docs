##  Compliance Service Provider (CSP)

Compliance Service Providers perform compliance operations like Know Your Customer, Customer Identification, Banking Secrecy Act compliance, and Sanctions Screening.

##  Corridor

A corridor is a combination of source currency, destination country, destination currency, and transfer method.

##  Destination Country

A destination country is the country location of a receiver account. See [country codes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_3166-1_alpha-3).

##  Destination Currency

A destination currency is the currency unit for the receiver account. See [currency codes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_4217).
